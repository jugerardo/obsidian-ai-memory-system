# 90_Archive

Inactive projects, abandoned drafts, old reference material that's no longer current. Everything here is still searchable — it's just out of your active view.

## What to archive

- Projects that are done (shipped, cancelled, or paused indefinitely)
- Reference material that's been superseded
- Old MOCs you no longer maintain

## What NOT to archive

- Insights — those stay in `40_Insights/` even after the project they came from is archived. The whole point of the insight layer is that the knowledge outlives the project.
- Notes you might still need to pull from — keep them where they are.

## How to archive a project

Move the whole folder: `mv 20_Projects/MyProject 90_Archive/`. Update any active MOCs that linked to it. Insights linked from the project remain valid because they live elsewhere.
